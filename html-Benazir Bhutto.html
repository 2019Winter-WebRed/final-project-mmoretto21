BIOGRAPHY

Benazir Bhutto was born on June 21, 1953. she was the child of former premier Zulfikar Ali Bhutto. She inherited leadership of the PPP adter a military coup overthrew her father's government and won election in 1988, becoming the first female prime minister of a muslim nation. Bhutto returned to Pakistan in 1977, after attending university in the US. She was placed under house arrest after the military coup led my General Mohammad Zia ul-Haq overthrew her father's government. One year later, the elder Bhutto was hanged after his conviction on charges of authorizing the murder of an opponent. She inherited her father's leadership of the PPP. She moved to England in 1984, becoming the joint leader in exile of the PPP, then returend to Pakistan on April 10, 1986, to launch a nationwide campaign for open elections.
She Married a wealthy landowner, Asif Ali Zardari, in karachi on December 18, 1987. The couple had three children: son Bilawal and two daughters, Bakhtawar and Aseef.
Bhutto was killed when an assin fired shots and then blew himself up after an election campaign rally in Rawalpindi on December 27, 2007. The attack also killed 28 others and wounded at least another 100. She died after hitting her head on part of her vehicle Sunroof. She was buried at her family's masoleum in Garhi Khuda Bakhsh, the southern province of Sindh. She was buried alongside her father Zulfikar Ali Bhutto, Pakistan's first popularly elected prime minister who was exucted by hanging.
